Our Businesses

Explore Our Diverse Sectors

Mineral Exploration

We are leading sustainable copper mining and mineral exploration projects across Africa, utilizing advanced geological technologies and responsible extraction practices to unlock the continent's vast mineral wealth while ensuring environmental stewardship.

Fin-Tech

We are bringing innovative solutions to problems and gaps in Africa’s financial sector, with the strategic use of technology to foster economic prosperity, ease of financial transactions, and reliance.

Agri Business

Our interest is in mechanized production, trade, and exportation; breathing fresh air into the palm oil production sector from crude, local methods to mechanized, tech- driven processing.

Renewable Energy

Focusing on affordable, sustainable, and consistent energy supply, we bring advancement to the energy sector and relief to people while also reducing dependence on fossil fuels.

Real Estate

We are strategically taking advantage of diverse opportunities in the real estate sector by investing in properties and facilities that are of high-yielding capacity over the long term.

Comercial Marketplace

We are creating a relaxing and luxury marketplace for consumer goods and services, serving as an all-in-one destination for a broad spectrum of goods and products from several brands.
