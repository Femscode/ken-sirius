Founder & CEO

Visionary Entrepreneur | African Capitalist | Economic Development Advocate

Courage Omilo

Courage Omilo is a bold and visionary African entrepreneur driven by a deep-seated passion for African capitalism and the economic transformation of the continent. As the Chief Executive Officer of Ken Sirius NG Limited, he has committed his life's work to building sustainable ventures across key industries that are vital to Africa's growth: mining, production and processing, energy, agriculture, and equity investment.

His approach to African capitalism emphasizes private-sector-led growth, local value creation, and strategic investments that empower communities, create jobs, and stimulate innovation. He advocates for Africans not only to consume global products but to become creators and exporters of value, technology, and solutions that reshape global narratives.

Leadership Philosophy

African Capitalism

Courage is a firm believer in home-grown capitalism as the cornerstone for Africa's independence and prosperity. He views industries not just as extractive businesses but as gateways to industrialization—driven by transparency, sustainability, and community development.

His vision extends beyond profit to reshaping Africa's economic narrative, building a future where African nations are defined by prosperity, innovation, and self-reliance rather than dependency.

Key Sectors & Impact

Making Impact

  • Mining: Active player in Nigeria's solid mineral space, focusing on copper ore, zinc exploration and responsible sourcing
  • Production & Processing: Developing partnerships for processing plants to retain wealth locally and enhance global competitiveness
  • Energy: Investing in renewable energy and off-grid solutions aligned with SDG 7 for sustainable energy access
  • Agriculture: Supporting agribusiness, sustainable farming, and agro-processing for food security and wealth creation
  • Equity Investment: Empowering startups and businesses through capital, mentorship, and strategic guidance

Sector Expertise & Vision

Building sustainable ventures across Africa's key growth industries

Mining Sector

Under Courage's leadership, Ken Sirius NG Limited has become an active player in Nigeria's solid mineral space, with an emphasis on the exploration and responsible sourcing of copper ore, zinc, and other critical minerals.

He views mining not just as an extractive industry but as a gateway to industrialization—one that must be driven by transparency, sustainability, and community development.

Production & Processing

Courage is a staunch advocate for value-added production. He believes Africa must transition from being a raw-materials supplier to becoming a continent of manufacturers and processors.

Through Ken Sirius, he is developing partnerships to set up processing plants for agricultural and mineral commodities—ensuring more wealth is retained locally while enhancing the continent's competitiveness in global trade.

Energy Sector

Access to reliable and sustainable energy remains a major challenge across Africa. Courage is actively exploring investment opportunities in renewable energy, off-grid solutions, and energy infrastructure.

His energy vision aligns with SDG 7 – ensuring access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all, understanding that energy is the backbone of industrialization.

Agriculture

As part of his economic development mission, Courage is deeply invested in the agricultural sector, particularly in agribusiness, sustainable farming, and agro-processing.

He envisions a food-secure Africa that harnesses its arable land, youthful workforce, and indigenous knowledge to create wealth and end hunger, supporting smallholder farmers and developing modern farming techniques.

"My drive is not merely for profit—it is a mission to reshape Africa's economic narrative. I envision a future where African nations are no longer defined by poverty or aid, but by prosperity, innovation, and self-reliance."

Courage Omilo, Founder & CEO of Ken-Sirius Inc.

A Legacy in the Making

Courage Omilo's drive is not merely for profit—it is a mission to reshape Africa's economic narrative. He envisions a future where African nations are no longer defined by poverty or aid, but by prosperity, innovation, and self-reliance.
